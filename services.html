Our Services

Opening October 2026, Martens Pet Cremation Services will offer respectful transportation, cremation options, and memorial products for families across Central Alberta.

Transportation

If your beloved pet has passed away at home, Martens Pet Cremation Services will provide respectful home pick-up and transportation to the facility throughout Central Alberta.

Transportation Fees

Home pick-up is available throughout Central Alberta, with travel calculated by distance and timing.

Cremation Options

Families will be able to choose the arrangement that feels right for their beloved companion. Pricing is based on weight and the type of cremation selected, so please contact us for a future quote.

Private Cremation

When you choose private cremation, your beloved pet will be cremated on their own. No other animal will be in the cremator. You will receive your pet’s cremains back.

Request pricing

Individual Cremation

When you choose individual cremation, your beloved pet will be cremated in their own area with dividers to keep pets completely separate. You will receive your pet’s cremains back.

Request pricing

Communal Cremation

When you choose communal cremation, your pet is cremated with other beloved companions. Cremains are not returned with this option.

Request pricing
